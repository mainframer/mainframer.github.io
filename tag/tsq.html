CICS中查看TSQ的命令:CEBR
2012-05-27

可以用CEBR这个transaction来查看和删除TSQ信息。这里:

  • CEBR#0VJ是TSQ的名字;

  • Z300 是当前CICS region的名字。
    ```ruby CEBR TSQ CEBR#0VJ SYSID Z300 REC 1 OF 0 COL 1 OF 0 ENTER COMMAND ===>
    **** TOP OF QUEUE *****

    **** BOTTOM OF QUEUE ****

TS QUEUE CEBR#0VJ DOES NOT EXIST
PF1 : HELP PF2 : SWITCH HEX/CHAR PF3 : TERMINATE BROWSE
PF4 : VIEW TOP PF5 : VIEW BOTTOM PF6 : REPEAT LAST FIND
PF7 : SCROLL BACK HALF PF8 : SCROLL FORWARD HALF PF9 : UNDEFINED
PF10: SCROLL BACK FULL PF11: SCROLL FORWARD FULL PF12: UNDEFINED
```
