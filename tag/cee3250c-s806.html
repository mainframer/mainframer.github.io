CEE3250C S806 R=00000004
2012-04-11

新写了个程序，JCL提交完出现错误:

CEE3250C The system or user abend S806 R=00000004 was issued.

分析dump，发现原因是:程序不在loadlib里面 "load module is not found in the library".忘记promote程序到相应环境了!
